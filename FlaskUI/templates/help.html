Help

What's this system?

This system is a simple Chinese Word Segmentation System, mainly based on HMM model.

It helps segment the Chinese text into seperated words.

How to use it?

In fact, the usage is quite easy!

Click 'Start' on th navbar and you will definitely know what to do.

More settings?

Easy, too.

Just click 'Settings' on th navbar to personalize the settings.

One thing to note: as there's no account system yet, your setting will only be stored loclly.

That means your settings won't last long, sadly.
